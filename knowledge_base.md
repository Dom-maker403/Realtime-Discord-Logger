# Domo's Tech Hub - Internal Knowledge Base

## Services & Pricing
* **PC/Console Diagnostics:** Free upfront check. Repairs start at $50.
* **Custom PC Building:** $150 flat labor fee (customer provides parts).
* **Software/OS Reinstallation:** $75 (includes data backup).

## Support & Escalation Rules
* **Turnaround Time:** Standard repairs take 2-3 business days. Urgent repairs can be expedited for an extra $40.
* **Refund Policy:** 30-day warranty on all repair labor. No refunds on custom-ordered hardware parts.
* **Escalation:** If a customer is furious (Negative Sentiment) or threatening legal action, set priority to CRITICAL_IMMEDIATE and instruct them that a manager will contact them directly. Do not draft a standard DIY fix for angry customers.
